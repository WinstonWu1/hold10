Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Hold10 is a pause and recovery support tool. The current MVP is designed to keep your progress on your own device. Hold10 is not a health care provider, emergency service, gambling treatment provider, law firm, financial adviser, or crisis monitoring service.

Data Stored On Your Device

Hold10 stores progress data locally in your browser or app storage. This can include session counts, check-ins, recovery tally values, protection checklist choices, country setting, and other progress settings you enter.

This local progress data may be sensitive because it can relate to gambling urges, slips, recovery behavior, money-at-risk estimates, and support choices. Protect your device with a passcode or biometric lock if you do not want other people with device access to see it.

No Account Or Cloud Sync In The MVP

The current MVP does not create user accounts, operate a backend profile system, or sync your recovery data to Hold10 servers.

Export And Reset

The app may let you export local progress data to a file and reset local data on the device. If you delete browser storage, uninstall the app, or reset local data, that local progress may be lost.

Exported files are controlled by you. Be careful where you save or share them because they may contain sensitive recovery information.

No Monitoring Or Emergency Response

Hold10 does not monitor your activity, gambling behavior, mood, location, risk level, or safety in real time. Hold10 cannot detect an emergency, contact emergency services, notify another person, block a gambling transaction, or guarantee that gambling, self-harm, financial loss, debt, legal issues, or relapse will be prevented.

Support Links

Hold10 links to external support services, phone numbers, and self-exclusion resources. Those services are operated by third parties and may have their own privacy practices, terms, availability, and eligibility rules. If you contact a third-party service, information you provide to that service is governed by that service's policies.

Analytics

The current MVP does not include third-party analytics in the app code. Hosting providers or app stores may process technical access logs according to their own policies.

Health, Legal, And Financial Advice

Hold10 is not medical, mental health, legal, or financial advice. It does not diagnose, treat, prevent, mitigate, or cure any condition and is not a substitute for qualified professional support. If you are in immediate danger, may harm yourself or someone else, cannot stay safe, or need urgent help, contact local emergency services now. If you need gambling, addiction, mental health, legal, debt, or financial support, contact a qualified professional or official support service.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Hold10 adds accounts, sync, analytics, or other features. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, use the support contact listed in the app store listing or product page where you accessed Hold10.
